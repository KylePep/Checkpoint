Tournament Rules and regulations!

Anyone who comes into contact with the outside of the ring, is knocked out and cannot get on their feet by the count of 10, or gives up, automatically loses. If both fighters are knocked out and cannot get on their feet by the count of ten, the match is declared a draw. However, if this happens in the final round, the first one to stand up on both feet and state the entire sentence "I'm the Champion" is declared the winner.
